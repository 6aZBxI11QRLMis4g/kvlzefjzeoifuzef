Gorski Kotar - stunning wilderness!
http://www.croatianvillasforsale.co.uk/ Gorski Kotar - an unspoilt landscape in Croatia's best known mountain region, home to the Croatian Olympic skiing team in the winter, and to adventure and nature enthusiasts all year round. From rafting and cycling to fishing and hunting, there's plenty to keep everyone occupied. Spectacular landscapes feature crystal clear lakes, underground caves, panoramic mountain peaks and lush green forested valleys. Only 30 minutes from the Adriatic coast, Gorski Kotar has something for everyone!
